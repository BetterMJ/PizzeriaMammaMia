Margherita
Margherita is a classic to eat. It is very simple: Tomato sauce with Mozzarella topping.
Funghi
We love to have the funghi, since it is made for exotic tastes. The toppings are: Tomato sauce with Mozzarella on top, Mushrooms, Olives
Kangaroo-Style
This one is a rarity. Kangaroo-Style Pizza has: Kangaroo meat, Tomato sauce, Mozzarella, Pepperoni, Garlic
Detroit-Style
The Detroit-Style is very unique, no pizza is like it. It is a squared instead of a round Pizza and has a thick crust and inverted toppings. Our has following: Tomato sauce, Mozzarella, Pepperoni.
